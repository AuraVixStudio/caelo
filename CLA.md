property clean and relicensable (which protects the project and any future
stewardship), we ask every contributor to agree to this Contributor License
Agreement before their first contribution is merged.

## How to sign

This repository uses an automated CLA check on pull requests (for example,
**CLA Assistant**). The first time You open a pull request, the bot will post a
link asking You to confirm that You have read and agree to this Agreement; signing
is a one-time action recorded against Your account. Pull requests cannot be merged
until the CLA check is satisfied.

For corporate contributions covering multiple employees, contact the maintainers
to arrange an entity-level agreement.
